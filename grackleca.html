GRACKLES in SACRAMENTO, CALIFORNIA (2021)

Follow their adventures on Twitter #TheGrackleProject
Xango - Adult male
Leg bands: Light blue (left leg) / Yellow (right leg) - Banded in Jan 2021
He wins the prize for solving the fewest options on the log puzzlebox - he didn't solve any of the 4 options. He is one the top 2 grackles with the largest body size with a leg length of 52.6 mm

Flan - Adult female
Leg bands: Orange / Orange - Jan 2021
She was in the top 2 fastest grackles to get through all of the aviary tests - taking less than 4 weeks! She is the lightest grackle, weighing in at 96 grams

Churro - Adult male
Leg bands: Silver / Green - Jan 2021

Tembleque - Adult male
Leg bands: Yellow / Silver - Jan 2021
When he was temporarily in the aviaries, he was the fastest grackle to reverse a color preference - in 39 trials. In the wild, he liked to go toe to toe with the snakes in his territory!
